Location: Blackwood Mansion

Case Name: The Midnight Murder


  • First Entry: The Call

    Rain was falling like a warning from above when I arrived at Blackwood Mansion at 12:43 AM. Mr. Harold Lancaster, the proprietor, was discovered slumped over his whiskey glass in the opulent living room, dead. One gunshot wound to the chest. No one heard anything, the shaky butler said. Seven suspects. One murderer.
